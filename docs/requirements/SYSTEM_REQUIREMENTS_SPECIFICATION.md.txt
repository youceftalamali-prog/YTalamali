# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Purpose

This document defines the official Software Requirements Specification (SRS) for AuraPost AI.

It describes every functional and non-functional requirement that must be implemented before the platform reaches Production.

This document becomes the single source of truth for all future development.

---

## 1.2 Project Vision

AuraPost AI is an Enterprise AI SaaS platform that enables businesses to:

- Import products from global marketplaces.
- Analyze products using Artificial Intelligence.
- Generate marketing content automatically.
- Generate AI images and videos.
- Publish content to social media.
- Synchronize Shopify and WooCommerce stores.
- Manage subscriptions and billing.
- Monitor business analytics.
- Automate e-commerce workflows.

The platform must be secure, scalable, modular and production ready.

---

## 1.3 Business Goals

The primary business goals are:

- Reduce manual work for online sellers.
- Increase product research efficiency.
- Automate content creation.
- Automate social media publishing.
- Provide intelligent marketplace insights.
- Support multiple e-commerce platforms.
- Become an enterprise SaaS platform.

---

## 1.4 Scope

AuraPost AI includes:

- Authentication
- User Management
- Workspace Management
- Shopify Integration
- WooCommerce Integration
- Product Import
- AI Product Analysis
- AI Content Generation
- AI Image Generation
- AI Video Generation
- Social Publishing
- Billing
- Credits
- Analytics
- Queue System
- Notifications
- Marketplace Intelligence
- Settings
- Integrations

Out of Scope:

- ERP
- CRM
- Accounting
- Shipping Providers
- Warehouse Management

---

## 1.5 Target Users

The platform is intended for:

- Shopify Store Owners
- WooCommerce Store Owners
- Dropshippers
- E-commerce Agencies
- Marketing Teams
- Enterprise Companies

---

## 1.6 Definitions

Workspace:
A logical company inside AuraPost.

User:
A person belonging to a workspace.

Project:
A workspace operation.

Credits:
AI consumption units.

Provider:
External AI service.

Marketplace:
Any external commerce platform.

Store:
Connected Shopify or WooCommerce shop.

---

## 1.7 Document Rules

This document is mandatory.

Every future feature must be documented here before implementation.

Any undocumented feature cannot be considered part of AuraPost AI.
